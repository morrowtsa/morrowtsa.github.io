MEET THE OFFICERS

President: Nancy Nguyen

The symbol of her office is the gavel. As president, she presides over all regular and special meetings and promotes cooperation in carrying out the activities and work of our organization.

Vice President: Tristen Nollman

The symbol of his office is a star. His duty as vice president is to see that there is always a strong membership, a good work program and to be alert to the welfare of our association.

Secretary: Teresa Campos

The symbol of her office is the pen. It is her responsibility as secretary to keep accurate and proper records of all business and correspondence of our association.

Reporter: Anna Ho

The symbol of her office is the beacon tower. It is her duty as the reporter to see that our school, community and national association has a complete report of our organization's activities.

Treasurer: Eric Mogaka

The symbol of his office is the balanced budget. It is his duty as the treasurer to keep accurate records of all funds and see that our financial obligations are met promptly.

Sergeant at Arms: Twumasi Pennoh

The symbol of his office is the hearty handshake. It is his responsibility as the sergeant-at-arms to see that the assembly is comfortable and properly welcomed. It is also his duty to serve as doorkeeper for this organization.

CHAPTER ACTIVITIES

  • Every year, Morrow TSA holds a canned food drive to give to underprivileged families, just in time for Thanksgiving. This year, our chapter was able to raise 75 cans to give away! The homeless department was overwhelmed with the massive amount; they were so happy to accept all our donations.

  • Since the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society are Georgia TSA's state partner, our chapter takes part in the annual Pennies for Patients fundraiser. This fundraising lasted for a month. We set a goal, and our members EXCEEDED it! We are so proud of all members that participated and can't until next year to set an even higher goal!

  • Our community's assisted living home holds an end-of-the-year celebration to wish on the new year. The event is always its biggest, with a photobooth, snack bars, and its grand "balloon drop"! 10 of our members helped around with the event, and they can all say they had a great time.
