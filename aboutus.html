MEET THE OFFICERS

President: Nancy Nguyen

Nancy has been Morrow TSA's past Reporter for two years, serving from 2014 to 2016. Aside from TSA, Nancy is heavily involved in other extracurricular organizations; she serves as the current Vice President of BETA Club and the Vice President of National Honors Society. Nancy is Salutatorian of her class and has earned the title of a national Questbridge CollegePrep Scholar. She plans to attend the University of Georgia in the fall.

Vice President: Tristen Nollman

Tristen is the only junior on the Morrow TSA officer team. He also currently serves as the Treasurer for SADD and is on the Community Service Committee for BETA Club. Tristen is ranked 1st in his class. His hobbies include coding, listening to vinyls, and tinkering with motorcycles.

Secretary: Teresa Campos

Teresa is both greatly involved in extracurricular organizations and sports at Morrow. She currently serves as President of Morrow DECA and is the captain for both varisty swimming and women's' soccer. Teresa is a national POSSE Scholar and will be attending Brandeis University on a full tuition scholarship in the fall. In between her busy schedule, Teresa is still very dedicated to her studies. She is a finalist of the Princeton Prize for Race Relations.

Reporter: Anna Ho

Anna is a very involved student, serving as the current President of BETA Club and the Treasurer of National Honors Society. In addition to extracurriculars, Anna is still very much dedicated to her academics. She is Valedictorian of her class and has earned the title of a national Questbridge CollegePrep Scholar and Questbridge Finalist. She plans to attend Georgia Institute of Technology in the fall.

Treasurer: Eric Mogaka

Eric currently serves as the President of National Honors Society and Vice President of SADD. Eric is a 21st Century State Ambassador and has attended Berry College's EarthCare and Emory University's SYLI (both hosted by 21st CL). He has also attended Boys State in the summer of 2016.

Sergeant at Arms: Twumasi Pennoh

Twumasi is currently SADD's Secretary. Twumasi has attended multiple summer programs, including Berry College's EarthCare, Morehouse College's Race to the Top Program, and Clark Atlanta University's Robotics Program.
