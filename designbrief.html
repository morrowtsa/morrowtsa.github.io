Augmented Reality Technologies

Augmented reality (AR) is when computer graphics or digital information is added to the real, physical world in real time. Augmented reality is a combination of both virtual reality and the real world. It enhances what we see, hear, feel, and smell. Currently, noticeable uses of augmented reality can be found in cell phones, cars, and video games.
