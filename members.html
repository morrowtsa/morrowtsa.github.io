Senior's Section

SENIORS! If you are interested in acquiring a TSA cord, the requirements are as follows:
Score a 70% or higher on the Statesmen Test (see below)
Attend ALL meetings (maximum 2 missed, with excuse)
Attend to atleast 1 workshops
Participation in chapter activites and events

Statesmen Test Provided to you is a study guide and a practice test. The Statesmen Test will be given out on a single day after school for all seniors to take. This date is yet to be announced, but anticipate it to be during April. If you do not receive a high enough score the first time, you have unlimited tries (you will need to set up a day with the officers) until you get a desired score. Happy studying!
